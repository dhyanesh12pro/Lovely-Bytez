# SEO Launch Checklist

Use this checklist before and immediately after launching the Lovely Bitez
website on the real production domain.

## Before Launch

- Confirm the exact business name: `Lovely Bitez`.
- Confirm the exact address:
  `No. 10, Kavarai Street, Kundrathur, Thirumudivakkam, Chennai, Tamil Nadu 600069, India`.
- Confirm the phone number: `+91 74485 56600`.
- Confirm opening hours before changing the editable text in `data/site.ts`.
- Replace temporary public-reference images with owner-approved original photos.
- Confirm menu items and current prices before adding any prices.
- Confirm whether WhatsApp should be enabled before adding a WhatsApp link.
- Set `NEXT_PUBLIC_SITE_URL` to the real domain.
- Keep preview and staging deployments blocked from indexing.

## Technical SEO

- Confirm every page has exactly one clear H1.
- Confirm every page has a unique title and meta description.
- Confirm canonical URLs use the real production domain.
- Confirm Open Graph previews show the correct business name and image.
- Confirm `/sitemap.xml` loads.
- Confirm `/robots.txt` allows the real production site.
- Confirm preview/staging `/robots.txt` blocks indexing.
- Test mobile performance and layout at 360px, 768px, 1024px, and 1440px.
- Check all images have meaningful alt text.
- Check all phone, map, Instagram, and order links.

## Structured Data

- Validate Restaurant / FoodEstablishment JSON-LD.
- Validate WebSite JSON-LD.
- Validate Breadcrumb JSON-LD.
- Validate Menu JSON-LD on `/menu`.
- Do not add review, rating, award, branch, or price schema unless verified.

## After Deployment

1. Connect the real domain.
2. Verify Google Search Console and submit:

```txt
https://www.your-real-domain.com/sitemap.xml
```

3. Claim and verify Google Business Profile.
4. Keep the same name, address, phone, hours, and website URL everywhere.
5. Upload real shop and food photos regularly.
6. Ask genuine customers for honest Google reviews and reply professionally.
7. Monitor Search Console and update the site, menu, and business information regularly.

## Do Not Do

- Do not promise or claim number-one Google rankings.
- Do not use fake reviews.
- Do not publish copied reviews as testimonials.
- Do not add fake ratings or aggregate rating schema.
- Do not keyword-stuff page copy.
- Do not create doorway pages for fake service areas.
- Do not buy backlinks.
- Do not publish other branch details until verified by the business.
